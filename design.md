# Ideas
- Algebraic terminology: *kitab al-jabr*
- Arabic is terser than English, maybe we can use sentences or more than one word (sounds difficult to parse)
- Add support for rhetorical algebra
- Use بتراء for unterminated string

# Challenges
- Uppercase letters do not exist in Arabic, and generally words in Arabic cannot be directly concatenated without affecting how the words look. Camel case is not an option for this language
- Diacritics should not be necessary for expressing the language. This is generally okay, but may make certain keywords hard to understand at first
